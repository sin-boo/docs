## Finding the AI Assistant

The AI assistant panel is located at the **top of your workspace**, alongside other docker panels like the color wheel and brushes.

<Tip>
  Look for the AI icon in your top toolbar to open the assistant panel!
</Tip>

## Setting Your API Key

<Steps>
<Step title="Get your Google Studio API key">
  Visit [Google AI Studio](https://makersuite.google.com/app/apikey) and create a free API key.
</Step>

<Step title="Enter your API key">
  In the AI assistant panel, paste your API key into the input field and click save.
  
  <Check>
  You should see a confirmation that your API key is connected.
  </Check>
</Step>

## Privacy & Security

Your API key is stored locally in Neutral Art and never sent to our servers. All communication happens directly between your installation and your chosen AI provider (Google Studio, OpenAI, or OpenRouter).

<Tip>
  Use the **hide button** in the AI panel when screen sharing or recording to keep your API key private!
</Tip>
